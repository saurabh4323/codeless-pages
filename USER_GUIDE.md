### 🚀 Codeless: Complete Platform Route & Feature Manual

Welcome to the definitive guide for **Codeless**. This document detail every accessible URL and feature across all user roles. Use this for documentation, orientation, or as a reference for your internal Word guides.

### 🌐 Global Public Routes

#### 🏠 Home Landing
**URL:** `codelesspages.com/`
The primary pitch page explaining the platform’s value. It highlights no-code simplicity, the 3-step launch process, and enterprise-grade performance for everyone from solopreneurs to startups.

#### 💳 Subscription & Plans
**URL:** `codelesspages.com/plans`
A dedicated pricing portal featuring Starter, Pro, and Enterprise tiers. Integrated with Stripe, it allows organizations to scale their seats and content volume instantly.

#### 📖 Content Upload Manual
**URL:** `codelesspages.com/manual`
A visual tutorial and rule-set for creating pixel-perfect content. It guides users through heading selection, color palettes, and media optimization for the best landing page results.

#### 📬 Professional Mailer
**URL:** `codelesspages.com/email`
A high-deliverability interface for sending one-off professional emails. Perfect for direct outreach or support communication using the platform's verified sender reputation.

### 👑 Superadmin Suite (Global Operations)

#### 🔑 Token Management
**URL:** `codelesspages.com/superadmin/tokens`
The master key generator. Superadmins create the unique organization tokens required for new administrators to claim their organizational workspace and start building.

#### 🎩 Administrator Directory
**URL:** `codelesspages.com/superadmin/admins`
A global list of every organization head. Superadmins can monitor admin activity, verify organizational status, and manage administrative credentials system-wide.

#### 👥 Global User Search
**URL:** `codelesspages.com/superadmin/users`
The ultimate directory for every registered user on the platform. Allows for cross-tenant search, support investigations, and global user management.

#### 🏗️ Master Template Bank
**URL:** `codelesspages.com/superadmin/templates`
The global library where all page structures are defined. Superadmins can push new layouts to all organizations or manage existing core templates.

#### ❓ Global Question Bank
**URL:** `codelesspages.com/superadmin/questions`
Allows Superadmins to manage the standardized question types available to admins. Ensures data consistency and high-quality collection across the entire platform.

#### 📥 All-Response Viewer
**URL:** `codelesspages.com/superadmin/responses`
A bird's-eye view of all data being submitted across the platform. Enables high-level auditing and data integrity checks across all tenants.

#### 🏢 Organization Overview
**URL:** `codelesspages.com/superadmin/organization`
A centralized view of all active tenants. Track which organizations are growing fastest and monitor the health of every branded ecosystem on the platform.

#### 📑 Global Page Monitor
**URL:** `codelesspages.com/superadmin/pages`
Track every single live page generated by the Acordial engine. Helpful for content moderation and monitoring system-wide bandwidth and performance.

### 💼 Admin Console (Organizational Management)

#### 🛡️ Org Setup & Verification
**URL:** `codelesspages.com/admin/setup`
The entry gate for new admins. Requires a unique organization token to unlock the registration process, ensuring that every organization is secure and isolated.

#### 📈 Hero Analytics Dashboard
**URL:** `codelesspages.com/admin/hero`
The visual heartbeat of the organization. Features real-time charts for member growth, submission rates, and quick shortcuts to all management tools.

#### 👥 Member Management
**URL:** `codelesspages.com/admin/users`
The full directory of a specific organization's members. Admins can invite new users, manage existing profiles, and track specific member contributions.

#### 📧 Email Sequences (Automation)
**URL:** `codelesspages.com/admin/email-sequences`
The marketing engine. Admins can design automated follow-up sequences, drip campaigns, and scheduled notifications to keep their members engaged and informed.

#### 🏗️ Content Manager (Templates)
**URL:** `codelesspages.com/admin/tempall`
Manage the specific subset of templates available to your organization. Hide unnecessary layouts and curate a streamlined experience for your members to follow.

#### ❓ Custom Form Builder
**URL:** `codelesspages.com/admin/questions`
The data architecture tool. Create custom question sets and form requirements that members must complete when using organizational templates.

#### 📤 Response Investigator
**URL:** `codelesspages.com/admin/responses`
Deep-dive into the data collected from your members. View, filter, and analyze submissions to make informed business decisions for the organization.

#### 📂 Media Review Portal
**URL:** `codelesspages.com/admin/uploads`
A specialized view for all files and images submitted by members. Admins can approve or reject uploads to ensure brand quality across all published pages.

#### 💬 Feedback Hub
**URL:** `codelesspages.com/admin/feedback`
Monitor member satisfaction. This portal collects sentiments and direct feedback from users, allowing admins to improve their organizational content and flow.

#### 🏠 Personal Dashboard

#### 📂 Template Library

#### 💬 Help & Contact
**URL:** `codelesspages.com/user/contact`
